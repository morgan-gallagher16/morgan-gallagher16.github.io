Picadillo

Meat and Raisins, Who Knew

Picadillo is a traditional dish in Latin America and the Philippines. Its main components consist of ground beef, tomatoes, raisins, and olives. Picadillo is mostly served with rice although the meat mixture is perfect to use as a filling as well. As you can imagine there are many variations of this dish. The following recipe has taken inspiration from Cuba, although this recipe is quite generic.


Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Garlic
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Green olives
  • Raisins
  • Cumin
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Sautee the onion, bell pepper, and garlic in olive oil until softened
  2. Add ground beef to skillet and cook until browned
  3. Stir in tomato sauce and let simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Add in olives, raisins, cumon, and salt
  5. Cook the mixture on low for 10 mintues
  6. Serve over rice
