A southern classic

Shrimp and grits is a classic southern confort food typically found in the Carolinas and Georgia. It consists of shrimp, grits, and a combination of different seasonings and add-ins including bacon, cheese, onions, and peppers. Below is a basic recipe for shrimp and grits.


Ingredients

  • Whole milk
  • Stone-ground grits
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Shrimp
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Bring the milk to a boil and add butter, salt
  2. Add the grits and whisk together over a simmer for 10 minutes
  3. Cook the shrimp in a pan, season with whatever you like
  4. Add the cheese in and continue to whisk until melted
  5. Adjust thickness of grits to your liking by adding more milk to loosen the grits
  6. Serve the grits with the shrimp
